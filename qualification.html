Qualifications

Reconstructive science goes hand in hand with maxilofacial science. To apply for the training programme you must have five 4-9 grade GCSEs including all three sciences. You must also have 3 high grade A levels and a degree in Dental technology.Then you should work as a Dental Technologist for a few years. Finally then can you apply for the NHS Reconstructive Scientist!
